**Abstract**

Direct Preference Optimization (DPO) has emerged as a powerful method for aligning large language models (LLMs) with human preferences. However, standard DPO may not fully capture the relationship between response length and reasoning quality. In this paper, we propose Length-Enhanced DPO (LE-DPO), which explicitly incorporates length information into the preference optimization process. Our experiments demonstrate that LE-DPO significantly improves reasoning performance on benchmarks such as GSM8K and MATH, particularly for problems requiring longer chain-of-thought reasoning.
