**Abstract**

The number 2 is a pretty good number. It comes after 1 and before 3, which is a pretty good position. In this paper, we discuss the merits of the number 2 and why it is such an important number in our lives. We also discuss some of the interesting properties of the number 2, such as being even and being the only even prime number.
