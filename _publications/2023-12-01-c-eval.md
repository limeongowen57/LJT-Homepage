**Abstract**

We introduce C-Eval, a comprehensive multi-level, multi-discipline Chinese evaluation suite for foundation models. C-Eval covers a wide range of subjects and difficulty levels, providing a holistic assessment of Chinese language understanding and reasoning capabilities.

The evaluation suite has become a standard benchmark for evaluating Chinese foundation models and is widely used by the research community.
