**Abstract**

We investigate the perception bottleneck of Vision-Language Models (VLMs) for chart understanding. Despite impressive capabilities on many vision-language tasks, current VLMs still struggle with chart understanding. We systematically study the perception capabilities of VLMs and find that the visual perception of charts is a major bottleneck limiting overall performance.

Through careful analysis, we identify specific perception challenges and propose approaches to improve the chart perception capabilities of VLMs.
