**Abstract**

In this work, we explore the composition of parameter-efficient modules through arithmetic operations. We show that these modules, which are trained to specialize in different tasks or capabilities, can be combined using simple arithmetic operations to create new capabilities or improve performance.

Our approach provides a flexible and efficient way to compose specialized modules, enabling new possibilities for modular adaptation and multi-task learning.
