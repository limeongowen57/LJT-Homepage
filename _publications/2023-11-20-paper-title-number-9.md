This paper reviews recent advances in CRISPR-based gene therapy for treating genetic disorders, discussing challenges and future prospects.

**Abstract**

CRISPR-Cas9 technology has revolutionized the field of gene therapy, offering precise genome editing capabilities for treating genetic disorders. This review summarizes recent progress in CRISPR-based therapeutic approaches, highlighting successful clinical applications and remaining challenges. We discuss delivery methods, off-target effects, immune responses, and ethical considerations, providing a comprehensive overview of the current state and future directions of gene therapy.
