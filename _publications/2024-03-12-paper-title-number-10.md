This paper reviews recent progress in renewable energy technologies, including solar, wind, and energy storage systems.

**Abstract**

The transition to renewable energy sources is crucial for addressing climate change and ensuring sustainable development. This review summarizes recent advances in key renewable energy technologies, including photovoltaic solar cells, wind turbines, and energy storage systems. We discuss improvements in efficiency, cost reductions, and challenges for widespread adoption, providing insights into the future of renewable energy and its role in the global energy landscape.
