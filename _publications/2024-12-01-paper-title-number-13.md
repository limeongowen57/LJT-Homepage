This paper presents blockchain solutions for enhancing supply chain transparency and traceability across multiple industries.

**Abstract**

Supply chain transparency has become increasingly important for consumers, regulators, and businesses. Blockchain technology offers immutable, decentralized record-keeping that can significantly improve traceability in complex supply chains. This paper presents practical blockchain solutions implemented across food, pharmaceutical, and luxury goods industries. Our case studies demonstrate significant improvements in traceability efficiency, reduction of counterfeit products, and enhanced consumer trust. We also discuss implementation challenges and provide recommendations for organizations considering blockchain adoption.
