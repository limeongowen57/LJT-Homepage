**Abstract**

Gravitational waves offer a unique window into the early universe, carrying information about events that occurred during the inflationary epoch and subsequent phase transitions. In this paper, we analyze potential signatures of primordial gravitational waves, discussing their detectability by current and future observatories. Our findings contribute to the growing body of research aimed at understanding the fundamental nature of our universe through gravitational wave astronomy.
