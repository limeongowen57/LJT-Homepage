**Abstract**

Early detection of neurodegenerative diseases such as Alzheimer's and Parkinson's is crucial for effective treatment and management. In this study, we identify and validate novel biomarkers in cerebrospinal fluid and blood that show high sensitivity and specificity for detecting these diseases at early stages. Our findings, validated across multiple patient cohorts, offer promising new avenues for diagnostic development and therapeutic monitoring in neurodegenerative diseases.
